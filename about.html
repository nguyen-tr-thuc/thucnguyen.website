Recent Data Analyst project

NYC Taxi Trip Time Prediction

Objective: Built a Regression model to predict the ride duration of taxi trips in NYC.

The untuned model was able to explain 68% of the variance on the test set, while the tuned model explained 74% of variance on the test set.

Algorithms such as Linear Regression, XG Boost, and Hist Gradient Boosting Regressor were used to predict trip_duration.

Hist Gradient Boosting Regressor provided the best results with an R2 score of 0,74 and RMSE of 3,37.
